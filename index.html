What is Pomodoro?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.

Learn more about Pomodoro

How Pomodoro Works?

  • Decide on the task to be done.
  • Start the pomodoro timer.
  • Focus on the task.
  • 1 Pomodoro is completed, when the timer expires.
  • After each pomodoro completed, take a short break.
  • When 4 pomodoros completed, take a long break.
  • Complete pomodoros as you need!
